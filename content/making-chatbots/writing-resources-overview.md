# Tropes, Archetypes, and Character Traits

Here are a few resources about tropes and archetypes! These are a good overview of general character personalities and storylines which you can use as a launching point for a bot.

But before we get started, what are they?

**Trope:** A reoccurring theme, motif, or plot device frequently used in a particular genre of art or literature, essentially a commonly used pattern or character archetype that can be recognized by audiences; it can refer to a specific situation, character trait, or image that appears repeatedly in storytelling.

**Archetype:** A recurrent symbol or motif in literature, art, or mythology. (Personally, I think of an archetype as a general characterization of a personality that reoccurs in media.)

**Character Traits:** Enduring qualities that define how individuals think, feel, and behave. They reflect a person’s values, attitudes, and moral compass, influencing their decisions and interactions.
