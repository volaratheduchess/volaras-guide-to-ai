# [A Complete List of Book Tropes: Everything You Need to Know](https://kindlepreneur.com/book-tropes/)

**My Analysis:** Despite the title, this list is not complete. It **is** comprehensive, but there are so many tropes out there that it is nearly impossible to cover them all! I do appreciate how the author separated the tropes based on genre, which makes the article easier to navigate and easier to apply.

# [101 Character Tropes All Screenwriters Should Know](https://thescriptlab.com/blogs/40182-101-character-tropes/)

**My Analysis:** I know the “Screenwriters” bit may put some off, but don’t let that fool you! These character tropes, otherwise known as archetypes, apply outside of movies! They can be seen in books, songs, even real life! This list has a lot of archetypes listed. They may not be very in-depth descriptions, but it’s enough to springboard from and maybe get some ideas.

# [150 Romance Novel Tropes](https://eviealexanderauthor.com/150-romance-novel-tropes/)

**My Analysis:** I like this list because it gives a bit of caution in using tropes, as well advice in how to use them well. They also categorize the tropes based on the relationships, which makes it easier to find and understand how they would work in context.
