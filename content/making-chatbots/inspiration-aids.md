# [Plot Generator](https://www.plot-generator.org.uk/cure-writers-block/)

**My Analysis:** This website was a really good find! I like how they have two different options: one, if you’re starting from scratch, and two, if you have characters in mind. You may be wondering why multiple characters would be useful, however if you’re doing a multi-bot or a world, it could be applied to bring them together!

# [Squibler](https://www.squibler.io/plot-generator)

**My Analysis:** This is a pretty basic plot generator. Easy to use and I probably wouldn’t have included it if not for the second option: Dangerous Mode. **WARNING:** This forces you to type! Set the time duration and you *have to keep typing or else all progress is lost*. You may be wondering why I would include this, but sometimes you just need to force yourself to write. I wouldn’t recommend using this setting for anything very important or consequential, but more as a practice exercise in writing, not going back and editing. Just let the words flow from your fingers and don’t get stuck on the details. You never know what might happen! But I suggest setting the timer low in case you end up writing something you wish to use. Better safe than sorry.

# [Writing Prompt Generator](https://selfpublishing.com/writing-prompts-generator/)

**My Analysis:** I really like this generator. It’s helpful how they divide the prompts by genre. This is helpful because it’s more specific and tailored to the story that you want to write. There is also an option to give a sentence starter, however it’s completely random and may not apply to your scenario. They also break down what the genres are on the main page, which is helpful.

---

# [Random Word Generator](https://randomwordgenerator.com/)

**My Analysis:** This website generates a random word. It has a few notable features, my favorite being the type of word you generate. You can choose from nouns, verbs, adjectives, extended, or Non-English, which they give you language options. This can be useful if you’re looking for an idea or direction for your work. Maybe a word will inspire you, or you don’t know what it means so you look it up and that inspires you. There are other features which the website explains, which is also helpful for navigating.
