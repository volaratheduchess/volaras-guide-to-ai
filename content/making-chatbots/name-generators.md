# [Behind the Name](https://www.behindthename.com/random/)

**My Analysis:** This resource is INCREDIBLE. You can customize what language you want the names from, if they’re from mythology and what ethnicity that mythology is, and even miscellaneous categories like Fantasy or Arthurian and **wrestler**. I mean, enough said! I was incredibly surprised by this find and am very excited to use it.

---

# [Character Name Generator](https://blog.reedsy.com/character-name-generator/)

**My Analysis:** Much like the previous link, this website separates the names into their ethnicities, though they offer less options. But on the other hand, they use adorable images that help you navigate the page easier. They also explain the meaning behind the names, which is another perk. This site has options for applied names under the Medieval, God, Fantasy, and Archetype categories, each with their own image card for reference.

---

# [Name Generator](https://www.namegenerator.co/)

**My Analysis:** This site is somewhat simple and easy to navigate while having a lot of options. From Towns to Technology to Occupation Names, there are plenty of options to choose from. Thankfully, they organize it decently well.

---

# [Random Name Generator](https://thestoryshack.com/tools/)

**My Analysis:** This website has a lot of options for name generators. Overwhelmingly so. A lot of them are random and things you wouldn’t expect, such as Star Wars Name Generator or Car Name Generator, but some are useful such as Tiefling Name Generator or City Name Generator. I suggest you explore and if you need a specific name, they probably have a generator for it.
