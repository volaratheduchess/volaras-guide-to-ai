The idea of basic prompting is to use natural language to create a description of the image you want to create. Much like in books, the AI uses the words you write to create the image. The AI cannot read your mind, so it may not be perfect, so if you have a specific idea in mind, it is best to be more specific as you describe to be as accurate as possible. If there are gaps, the AI will likely take liberties and fill them as best as it can.

# What is Basic Prompting?

Basic prompting is just what it sounds like: one of the most basic ways to prompt an AI image. It is one of the best methods to use for beginners because of its simplicity. It works well for simple scenes or objects but can be used for more complicated images as well. All that you have to do is describe what you want in plain words.  
  
Example: “A red fox sitting under a tree in the snow.”

![](assets/ai-art-resources-image-prompting-basic-prompting-32.jpg)![](assets/ai-art-resources-image-prompting-basic-prompting-33.jpg)

![](assets/ai-art-resources-image-prompting-basic-prompting-34.jpg)![](assets/ai-art-resources-image-prompting-basic-prompting-35.jpg)

As you can see, Bing gave us just what we asked for: a red fox sitting under a tree in the snow. It mostly kept the style consistent with fantasy/hyper realism and that is because DALL·E 3, the model behind Bing’s generator, was trained on **billions of text–image pairs** scraped from the web. The majority of those pairs are **photographs or photorealistic stock images** (vs. paintings, anime, abstract art). Since models learn from frequency, “realistic” is the *default baseline* for a vague prompt.

Now let's try a few more images for a baseline before we start getting into style tags. I will be using these basic images as a reference point to demonstrate the changes as we go through new techniques.  
  
When it comes to basic prompting, words at the beginning of the prompt hold more weight than those at the end. This can help you determine what order your prompt should go in.

> A man with short dark hair and blue eyes. He has tattoos beneath his white t-shirt. He wears jeans and black boots as he sits on a bench.

# Adding Art Styles

Once you have the prompt figured out, the next step is art style. If you have a specific art style in mind, great! If you don’t I encourage you to look at the sites below or go out and look at some art you like and use those styles.

[70+ AI art styles to use in your AI prompts](https://zapier.com/blog/ai-art-styles/?msockid=1e9158ebc3b86e462df44b86c2da6fdb#medium)

[Exploring AI Art Styles: A Comprehensive List with Examples](https://www.aiartkingdom.com/post/ai-art-styles-list)

Be aware that not all allow photorealistic or hyper-realistic art, even if it is AI generated.
