# Image Generation

As you see more AI art, you may notice different styles of the art, the consistency with which one makes their art, and other things like how accurate it is. The reason for these differences is not only because of the style of prompting, but it can also be as simple as the different platforms used! There are *countless* AI image generators out there and I'll briefly go over a lot of them here.  
  
I will also go over prompting, editing tools and features that sites offer, and other resources you may need on your AI creation journey.
