## Directional Lighting

These define where light originates and how shadows fall.

- Overhead spotlight

- Top-down theatrical lighting

- Low-angle underlighting

- Side lighting (split lighting)

- 45° key light

- Backlighting / rim lighting

- Edge lighting

- Silhouette lighting

- Window light from left/right

- Three-point studio lighting

- Butterfly lighting (beauty lighting)

- Rembrandt lighting (triangle cheek highlight)

---

## Intensity & Contrast Behaviors

These control depth and drama.

- High contrast chiaroscuro

- Soft diffused lighting

- Hard shadow lighting

- Sharp shadow falloff

- Deep shadow pockets

- Low contrast ambient wash

- Balanced exposure

- Blown-out highlights

- Crushed blacks

- Dramatic shadow gradients

---

## Light Quality / Texture

These affect surface realism.

- Volumetric light beams

- Dust-lit rays

- Hazy atmosphere diffusion

- Fog-scattered light

- Reflective bounce lighting

- Subsurface scattering (skin glow)

- Specular highlights

- Glossy surface reflection

- Matte light absorption

- Caustic light patterns (water reflections)
